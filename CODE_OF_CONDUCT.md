# Code of Conduct

BinaryTriage-X is maintained as a professional security engineering project.

Contributors are expected to:

- communicate respectfully
- provide technically grounded feedback
- protect private and sensitive information
- avoid distributing malicious or unauthorized material
- follow responsible disclosure practices
- respect applicable laws, licenses, and research ethics

Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, deliberate disruption, or unsafe handling of malware and sensitive data are not acceptable.
